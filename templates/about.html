Idea of Website

In 2019, the new coronavirus (COVID-19), also known as the "Wuhan Pneumonia" epidemic, continues to develop. Masks have become a necessity, and other protective equipment has also become a material that the public has snapped up. Many people choose to reuse masks because they cannot buy masks. There are also many low-income families who cannot afford masks. Due to increased demand, most masks are becoming more expensive, so they choose to purchase some inferior masks. The protection efficiency of these low-quality masks is very low, and even increases their chances of contracting bacterial diseases. Therefore, I hope to establish an online shopping platform to sell some anti-epidemic products to different users at a normal price and establish a reservation function for users to choose. Avoid panic buying situation so that every user can buy a mask to spend this dangerous period.
